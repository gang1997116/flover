Our Story

The Flover brand was founded by three women: Xintong, Yinggang and Yi. The brand name means "For lovers". They first studied design and digital media before moving on to the floral industry. Starting from this, they embarked on a passionate floral career. They love to share their feelings about flowers with others and to be passionate. This is why they often participates in floral art seminars and performance shows, walking at the forefront of the floral art world. In view of their past digital design experience, they decided to create an online flower shop, combined digital technology to create a virtual flower cultivation platform, in an attempt to regain everyone's interest in planting flowers, therefore Flover was born. Join us and love flowers.
